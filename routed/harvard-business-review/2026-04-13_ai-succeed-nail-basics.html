Executive Summary 執行摘要
Redman 指出，企業在追逐 AI 的過程中，往往忽略了最根本的能力建設。他提出五個歷久彌新、相互強化的基本原則：客戶中心、流程導向、數據驅動決策、持續改善、人本品質觀。這些原則並非 AI 時代的新發明，而是品質管理與卓越營運的核心智慧，但在 AI 導入的脈絡下被賦予了全新的意義。
Redman argues that companies chasing AI often neglect fundamental capability building. He presents five timeless, mutually reinforcing principles: customer centricity, process focus, commitment to acting on facts, continuous improvement, and a recognition that quality happens through people. These aren't new AI-era inventions but core quality management wisdom given fresh relevance in the AI context.

原則一：客戶中心 Customer Centricity

以客戶為中心不是口號，而是一種系統性的思維方式。在 AI 時代，這意味著：
Customer centricity isn't a slogan — it's a systematic way of thinking. In the AI era, this means:
  • 理解客戶真正的問題，而非急於用 AI 提供解方
  • 以客戶價值作為 AI 專案優先排序的依據
  • 用客戶回饋驅動模型迭代與流程優化
核心洞察 Core Insight
許多 AI 專案失敗，不是因為技術不行，而是因為解決了沒人在意的問題。客戶中心思維確保 AI 投入方向正確。
Many AI projects fail not due to technical inadequacy, but because they solve problems nobody cares about. Customer-centric thinking ensures AI investments address real needs.

原則二：流程導向 Focus on Process

AI 不是魔法 — 它在既有流程中運作。如果流程本身混亂、不可靠，AI 只會放大這些問題。
AI isn't magic — it operates within existing processes. If processes themselves are chaotic and unreliable, AI will only amplify those problems.
  • 先優化流程，再導入 AI 自動化
  • 端到端視角：理解上下游的依賴關係
  • 標準化是可擴展 AI 部署的前提
類比思考 Analogy
把 AI 加入糟糕的流程，就像在崎嶇不平的道路上裝上更強的引擎 — 速度更快，但失控的風險更高。
Adding AI to a bad process is like putting a more powerful engine on a rough road — faster speed, but higher risk of losing control.

原則三：數據驅動 Commitment to Acting on Facts

基於事實做決策是品質管理的基石，在 AI 時代更為關鍵。數據品質直接決定 AI 輸出的品質。
Commitment to acting on facts is a cornerstone of quality management, and becomes even more critical in the AI era. Data quality directly determines AI output quality.
  • 數據治理：建立可信賴的數據基礎設施
  • 消除偏見：主動檢測並修正數據中的系統性偏差
  • 度量文化：用數據驗證 AI 的商業影響
常見陷阱 Common Pitfall
「Garbage in, garbage out」的法則在 AI 時代被指數級放大。一個基於低品質數據訓練的模型，其錯誤會以規模化的方式傳播。
The "garbage in, garbage out" rule is exponentially amplified in the AI era. Errors from models trained on low-quality data propagate at scale.

原則四：持續改善 Continuous Improvement

持續改善（Kaizen）的精神在 AI 領域尤為重要。AI 模型需要不斷迭代、監控和調整。
The spirit of continuous improvement (Kaizen) is especially vital in AI. AI models require constant iteration, monitoring, and adjustment.
  • 模型監控：追蹤 AI 輸出品質的漂移
  • 回饋循環：建立用戶回饋到模型改進的閉環
  • 小步快跑：漸進式部署優於大爆炸式轉型
實務應用 Practical Application
建立「AI 營運看板」：
  • 模型準確率趨勢
  • 用戶採用率
  • 業務影響指標 (ROI)
  • 數據品質分數
Build an "AI Operations Dashboard" tracking model accuracy trends, user adoption rates, business impact metrics (ROI), and data quality scores.

原則五：人本品質 Quality Happens Through People

最終，品質是透過人來實現的。AI 是工具，但理解問題、設計解決方案、確保品質的是人。
Ultimately, quality happens through people. AI is a tool, but understanding problems, designing solutions, and ensuring quality — that's all human work.
  • 培訓投資：提升全員的 AI 素養
  • 跨職能協作：打破技術團隊與業務團隊的壁壘
  • 心理安全：鼓勵實驗與從失敗中學習
關鍵原則 Key Principle
Redman 強調，AI 專案的成敗取決於人，而非演算法。最好的模型在一個不信任數據、不理解流程的組織中也會失敗。
Redman emphasizes that AI project success depends on people, not algorithms. The best models fail in organizations that don't trust data or understand processes.

思考與練習 Practice Questions

Q1 概念理解 為什麼 Redman 強調這五個原則是「歷久彌新」而非「全新」的？
因為這些原則源自品質管理運動（TQM），已在製造業和服務業被驗證數十年。AI 只是讓這些原則變得更加迫切 — 糟糕的流程和數據在 AI 放大效應下會造成更大的損害。
Because these principles originate from the Total Quality Management (TQM) movement and have been validated in manufacturing and services for decades. AI simply makes them more urgent — poor processes and data cause greater damage under AI's amplification effect.
Q2 產業應用 以製藥業為例，如何應用「流程導向」原則來提升 AI 在藥物開發中的成功率？
在藥物開發中，AI 被用於靶點識別、化合物篩選和臨床試驗設計。「流程導向」意味著：先標準化數據收集流程（如 eCRF 格式一致性），再導入 AI 預測模型；端到端追蹤從靶點到 IND 申請的完整流程，找出瓶頸；建立 SOPs 確保 AI 輸出的可驗證性和合規性。
In drug development, AI is used for target identification, compound screening, and clinical trial design. "Process focus" means: standardize data collection processes (e.g., eCRF format consistency) before deploying AI prediction models; track the end-to-end pipeline from target to IND application to identify bottlenecks; establish SOPs ensuring AI output verifiability and compliance.
Q3 批判思考 「先優化流程再導入 AI」的建議是否過於保守？有沒有先用 AI 再優化流程的成功案例？
這是合理的批判。某些場景下，AI 可以先被用來「照亮」流程中的問題（如 process mining），然後再進行優化。但 Redman 的論點核心是：如果在流程混亂時就大規模部署 AI 自動化，風險遠大於收益。關鍵是區分「AI 作為診斷工具」和「AI 作為執行引擎」— 前者可以先行，後者需要流程基礎。
This is a fair critique. In some scenarios, AI can first be used to "illuminate" process issues (e.g., process mining) before optimization. But Redman's core argument is: deploying AI automation at scale within chaotic processes carries risks far exceeding benefits. The key distinction is between "AI as diagnostic tool" (can go first) and "AI as execution engine" (needs process foundations).
一句話總結：AI 不是跳過基本功的魔法，而是放大基本功效果的乘數。五大原則掌握得越好，AI 的回報就越高。
Bottom Line: AI isn't magic that bypasses fundamentals — it's a multiplier that amplifies them. The better you master the five principles, the higher your AI returns.
